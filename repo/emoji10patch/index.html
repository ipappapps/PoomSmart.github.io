For iOS 6.0+

Emoji10 Patch will add additonal emojis of those Unicode 9.0 to your keyboard. Insertion points of emojis are determined by the developer, not Apple themselves. Importantly, you need Unicode 9.0-compatible font in order to display those emojis correctly. You can find compatible fonts for it from vxbakerxv.github.io repository.


Emoji10 Patch won't work well with Emoji10, due to font limitation.
